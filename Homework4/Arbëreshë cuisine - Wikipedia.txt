 The  Arbëreshë cuisine  ( Albanian :  Kuzhina Arbëreshë ;  Italian :  Cucina Arbëreshë ) is the  cuisine  of the  Arbëreshë people  in  Italy . It has been significantly influenced by  Albanian  and  Italian cuisine . [1]
