   Ossetian cheese 
 Ossetian cuisine  ( Ossetian :  Ирон хæринæгтæ   Iron khɐrinɐgtɐ ) refers to the cooking styles and dishes of the  Ossetians .
 
 
 Ossetian pie [ edit ] 
   
   Fydzhin, Ossetian meat pie. 
   Sliced fydzhin 
 Fydzhin ( Ossetian :  Фыдджын ,  Russian :  Фыдджин ) is a type of meat pie.
 Three pies  ( Ossetian :  æртæ чъирийы ,  Russian :  три пирога ) is an important concept in Ossetian culture, representing  sun, earth and water. [1] 
 
 Ossetian beer [ edit ] 
   Ossetian beer , bagani 
 Beer has been prepared and enjoyed in Ossetia since ancient times. [2]   Eeron Bagany  is a famous beer festival celebrating this cultural treasure in a manner comparable to that of the German  Oktoberfest .
 
 Ossetian cheese [ edit ] 
 Ossetian cheese is a traditional  cheese  of Ossetians. [3]
