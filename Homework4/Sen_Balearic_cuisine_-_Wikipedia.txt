Balearic cuisine is a Mediterranean cuisine as cooked in the Balearic Islands, Spain.
It can be regarded as part of a wider Catalan cuisine, since it shares many dishes and ingredients with Catalonia and the Valencian Community.
Others view it as part of a more global Spanish cuisine.
Traditional Balearic cuisine is rich in vegetables, cereal and legumes as well as being low in fats.
