 Regional cuisine 
 Epirotic cuisine  ( Greek :  Ηπειρώτικη κουζίνα, or Κουζίνα της Ηπείρου ) is the traditional  Greek cuisine  of the region of  Epirus . It is based mostly on  dairy  products; butter and various cheeses. [1]
